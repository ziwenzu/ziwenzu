# Gary Ziwen Zu

Political science researcher studying authoritarian legality, legal intermediation, and governance in China.

## Focus

- Authoritarian institutions and state-society relations
- Legal aid, dispute resolution, and political behavior
- Field experiments, audit experiments, and survey experiments
- Computational social science, text workflows, and audio-based measurement

## Current Work

- Dissertation on how law becomes usable under authoritarian rule
- Research on public legal services, legal mobilization, and professional brokerage
- Empirical projects linking legal institutions, local governance, and political claims
